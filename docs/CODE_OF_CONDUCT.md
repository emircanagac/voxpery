# Voxpery Community Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Voxpery is committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Voxpery community will experience an environment free of harassment and discrimination.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and graciously accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behaviors:**
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Insulting or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks or aggressive behavior
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Trolling or spam
- Publishing private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by:
1. Opening an issue on GitHub
2. Contacting maintainers directly
3. Emailing: voxpery@gmail.com

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. We will treat all reports confidentially.

## Consequences

Community members who violate this code may face:
- Warning
- Temporary or permanent ban from the community
- Removal of contributions

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and forums
- Community chats (Discord/Matrix)
- Official events and meetups
- Anywhere representing the Voxpery community
